## Regression policy

Every accepted defect adds a deterministic regression test in its owning layer before the repair is archived.

`DSET-TEST-SKILL-004` is historical for the sixteen-entry topology, and
`DSET-TEST-SKILL-012` is absorbed by `DSET-TEST-SKILL-014`; both remain
immutable history.

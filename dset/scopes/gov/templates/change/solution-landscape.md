## Decision

Comparison preserves partial, tied, `incomparable`, `inconclusive`, and
`evidence-needed` results instead of forcing a winner. `sufficient`, `degraded`,
and `insufficient` describe whether the cited evidence may support the intended
comparison; they are not selection outcomes. Record adopt, adapt, or reject only
in a separate Decision for the declared context after sufficient applicable
proof. Build or defer may be next actions, not candidate verdicts. Do not
manufacture a universal winner score.

# Proof evidence — {{title}}

Store bounded, redacted evidence and disposable proof-of-fit outputs here. Each
promoted proof supports one claim for one intended use and records producer or
performed work, method/setup, repository/version/environment/assumption context,
observation time or validity window, exact commit or artifact version, evidence
address and polarity, currentness, reopen trigger, and attempted uses the proof
cannot support. For high-risk or reliance-bearing claims, also separate producer
from source maintainer, planned method from performed work/deviations, and record
credible rival explanations. Keep raw generated logs ephemeral unless a concise
excerpt is required to diagnose failure.

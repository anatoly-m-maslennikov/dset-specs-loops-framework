# Requirement — Content-role feedback cycle

A record may enter the cycle at any justified role. Its route follows its own
semantics rather than the preceding or following workflow step.

## Primary claim

Content roles form the navigational feedback cycle Inquiry to Definition to Rationale to Method to Implementation to Observation to Inquiry without determining artifact identity or authority.

## Rationale

A development flow is useful for entry gates and navigation but must not redefine the artifact routed through it.
